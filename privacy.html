PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: January 2026

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Sever Gallery collects only the minimum personal information necessary to conduct business operations, including but not limited to:

  • Full name
  • Shipping and billing address
  • Email address
  • Payment-related information (processed securely by third-party payment providers)

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal data beyond what is required for sales, shipping, customer communication, or legal compliance.

2. USE OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

Personal information is used solely for:

  • Processing orders and payments
  • Shipping and delivery of purchased artwork
  • Order confirmations and customer service communications
  • Compliance with legal and regulatory obligations

3. THIRD-PARTY SHARING

Sever Gallery does not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise monetize customer data.

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to provide website functionality, payment processing, order fulfillment, shipping, analytics, or other services on our behalf.

Personal data is shared strictly on a limited, need-to-know basis with trusted Third-Party Service Providers, which may include:

  • Shipping carriers (e.g., UPS, FedEx, DHL)
  • Payment processors (e.g., PayPal, Stripe)
  • Website hosting, analytics, and technical service providers
  • Accounting, legal, or compliance professionals when required by law

Third-Party Service Providers are granted access to personal data solely for the purpose of performing specific services on Sever Gallery's behalf. They are contractually and legally obligated not to disclose, sell, retain, or use such data for any other purpose.

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and Sever Gallery cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. DATA RETENTION

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR & CCPA)

In compliance with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR and CCPA, customers have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of their personal information
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities where applicable

Requests may be submitted via written communication to Sever Gallery. We will respond within the timeframes required by law.

7. PROMOTIONAL COMMUNICATIONS

Mailing List Subscribers: Individuals who voluntarily subscribe to Sever Gallery's mailing list expressly consent to receive periodic emails that may include information about products and services, exhibitions, promotions, or other gallery-related materials.

Purchasers: Customers who complete a purchase may receive limited promotional communications related to Sever Gallery's offerings in addition to required transactional communications.

Opt-Out: Recipients may opt out of promotional emails at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions included in the email or by contacting Sever Gallery directly.

8. COOKIES AND TRACKING

Sever Gallery uses cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to operate and improve its Website and communications. These technologies may be used to:

  • Enable core website functionality
  • Analyze website traffic and user interactions
  • Measure the effectiveness of email communications
  • Improve content, layout, and user experience

By using the Sever Gallery website, you consent to the use of cookies and tracking technologies as described in this Policy.

9. POLICY UPDATES

Sever Gallery reserves the right to update or modify this Policy at any time. Updates will be effective upon posting and apply prospectively.

By purchasing from or engaging with Sever Gallery, you acknowledge and agree to this Policy.
